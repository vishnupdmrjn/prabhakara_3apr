Treatments

Many specific treatments prescribed in old medical classics for some fatal illnesses are currently practiced only in Kerala. And they are effectively managed at all Prabakala AyurCare centers to meet the needs of modern patients.

  • Dhara
  • Navarakizhi

Dhara

Dhara is thought to help treat several illnesses, including stress-related problems, insomnia, and other degenerative brain ailments.

Patients lie on a unique wooden drone with the neck and head comfortably placed. Then, by a specially designed device called Dara Katti, an uninterrupted continuous flow of the prescribed drug solution is gently poured from the prescribed height onto his forehead for an hour to an hour and a half.

Navarakizhi

It is a unique massage that rejuvenates the body. It is effective in degenerative muscle diseases like poliomyelitis, muscular atrophy, etc. It is more effective when done immediately after Pizhichil treatment.

Navara rice is cooked by boiling it in cow's milk along with suitable ingredidient. The linen bags filled with rice are dipped in the same mixture, kept boiling over a gentle flame, and applied by masseurs at a comfortable temperature over the patient's whole body. The course of treatment can last for 14 or 21 days.

Sirovasti

Sirovasti is a necessary procedure that is found to be very effective in trigeminal neuralgia, hemicrania, optic atrophy, otalgia, deafness, facial paralysis, and all diseases affecting cranial nerves.

The patient wears a leather cap over their head to keep the recommended medicinal oil at a tolerable temperature. The cap is filled with oil up to one finger above the crown of the head. The treatment lasts for an hour to an hour and a half.

Pizhichil

This is effective in diseases connected with the Vata Dosha and its allied manifestations like spondylosis, hemiplegia, Arthritis, back pain, muscular dystrophy etc.

The sufferer is forced to lie on a Droni constructed of wood (a specially designed wooden plank). Then, from a precise height, pieces of linen dipped in a soothingly warm therapeutic oil are massaged over the patient's body. Four masseurs on either side of the patient rub them gently as the warm oil spreads throughout the body.

Nasyam

This treatment is specially effective in paralysis, rheumatism, arthritis, spondylosis, and other psychosomatic diseases.

This classical therapy is also made use of for devising new treatment modalities to deal with several severe ailments of the modern man, such as parkinsonism, motor neuron diseases, cancer, etc. The effective treatments provided at the hospital are Snehapanam Dhara.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have answered some of the most queried questions helping you have a better hospitality experience with Prabhakara.

  • What is the duration of the treatment each day? What do we do the rest of the day?

    The duration of each treatment is one hour, and if required, patients will schedule more treatments/ procedures each day. You have to take complete rest during the treatment period. Each room has Television. A multilingual library and garden with walking pathways are also there.

  • What should we bring when we come there for treatment?

    You should bring all the relevant medical reports and clothing for your daily use.

  • Please tell us about the climate. Should we bring any sweaters or rugs?

    The climate is moderate and warm clothes are not required in Hospitals in Kerala.

  • Can I undergo a rejuvenation therapy in your institution for a certain number of days? Whom should I contact?

    Yes, you can undergo rejuvenation therapy in our institution. You may kindly contact our doctors with a few more details regarding the online questionnaire for advice in this regard.

  • Is there any treatment to reduce the weight?

    A combination of appropriate medicines, special massages like Udwarthanam, medicated enema, and adequate restrictions in the diet are seen as effective in reducing weight. However, a drastic reduction cannot be expected. Individual conditions will decide the exact treatment modalities only after a personal examination.
